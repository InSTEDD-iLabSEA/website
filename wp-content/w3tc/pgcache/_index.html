About iLab Southeast Asia

The iLab Southeast Asia focuses on improving health, safety and development through a combination of human-centered design, agile development and cross sector collaboration.

Featured project

Verboice

Voice is the most universal and inclusive means of communication, and it’s an ideal way to expand the reach and impact of health and humanitarian technologies. Verboice is a free and open-source tool that makes it easy for anyone to create and run projects that interact via voice, allowing your users to listen and record messages in their own language and dialect or answer questions with a phone keypad.
